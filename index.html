A Tribute Page: Elon Musk Edition

Elon Musk

Timeline of Elon Musk: The World's Richest Man.

June 27th, 1971

Start of Life

Born on 28th June 1971 in South Africa. Elon Reeve Musk (born June 28, 1971) is a South African-born, Canadian-American business magnate, engineer, inventor and investor.

January 11th, 1991

Creation of Blastar Video Game

Elon Musk engineered a game in 1984 and named it "Blastar". The source code was published in a magazine in which he received $500 for.

August 15th, 1995

University of Pennsylvania

In 1995, Elon Musk received his Bachelor in Physics and Economics from the University of Pennsylvania.

September 4th, 1995

Moved to California, United States

In 1995, Elon Musk moved to California and applied for a Ph.D in Physics at Stanford University, however his journey at the program would only last two days.

October 31st, 1995

Queen University

Elon Musk was accepted into Queen's University.

February 15th, 1999

Zip2

With US$28,000 money borrowed from Musk's father, Musk, his brother Kimbal, and Greg Kouri started online software startup Zip2 in 1995. They set up shop in Palo Alto in a small rented office. For the newspaper publishing sector, the company created and sold an Internet city guide including maps, directions, and yellow pages. Musk claims that he couldn't afford an apartment before the company became popular, so he rented an office, slept on the couch, showered at the YMCA, and shared one computer with his brother.

February 28th, 1999

Compaq acquires Zip2

Musk received 7% or $22 million dollars from the sale.

March 13th, 2000

Co-Founded X.com

Musk co-founded X.com, an online financial services and e-mail payment company, with US$10 million from the sale of Zip2. The company was one of the first federally insured internet banks, with over 200,000 customers in its first months of existence. Musk's investors viewed him as inexperienced, and before the end of the year, he had been replaced by Bill Harris, the CEO of Intuit. To minimize competition, X.com merged with online bank Confinity the following year. Confinity, which was founded by Max Levchin and Peter Thiel, had its own money-transfer service, PayPal, which was more popular than X.com's.

May 16th, 2000

Marriage

Elon marries Justine in 2000, who he later seperated himself from in 2008, after having six sons.

October 1st, 2000

eBay buys PayPal

PayPal was acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion dollars in stock, in which Musk's share was $165 million dollars.

June 2nd, 2002

Space X

With $100 million of his early fortunes, Elon Musk founded Space Exploration Technologies (SpaceX)

February 4th, 2004

Tesla Motors Investment

Elon Musk led the Series A round of investment in February 2004, joining Tesla's Board of Directors as its Chairman.

February 9th, 2006

Solar City

Musk provided the initial brainwork and concept, along with the financial capital for SolarCity, who was co-founded by his cousins Lyndon and Peter Rive.

March 5th, 2010

Falcon 9 (SpaceX)

Elon Musk who was accompanied by President Barack Obama, successfully launched Falcon 9 in 2010.

June 11th, 2012

Tesla Model S

Elon Musk and his company, Tesla began delivery of its four-door electric vehicle, the Tesla Model S.

August 11th, 2013

Hyperloop

Musk revealed a proposal for a new form of transportation between the Greater Los Angeles Area and the San Francisco Bay Area

June 6th, 2021

Annoymous Targets

An annoymous messanger sends a message to Elon Musk after tweeting down the crypto market.

July 8th, 2021 @ 7:30PM

DOGECOIN

With the decline of Bitcoin, Elon Musk continues tweeting about Dogecoin and pushes the prices up.

BTC & ETH are pursuing a multilayer transaction system, but base layer transaction rate is slow & transaction cost is high.

There is merit imo to Doge maximizing base layer transaction rate & minimizing transaction cost with exchanges acting as the de facto secondary layer.

— Elon Musk (@elonmusk) July 9, 2021

April 14th, 2022

Elon's offer to buy Twitter

After becoming shareholder of Twitter, Elon Musk takes a next step and offers to buy 100% of Twitter Inc. The board of Twitter takes their time to decide whether to accept his offer. The Twitter stock price rose 18% after the annoucement was made.

I made an offer https://t.co/VvreuPMeLu

— Elon Musk (@elonmusk) April 14, 2022

April 24, 2022

Elon Musk: Owner of Twitter

After the Twitter board decided on the offer for more than 10 days, Elon receives approval from the shareholders of Twitter to buy the rest of all the shares of Twitter for $43 billion dollars. Elon Musk believes the platform has much more potential and expects to grow the platform immensly in the coming years.

October 28th, 2022

Twitter/ X Making it Official

After months of rumors and negotiation between Musk and Twitter, on Friday the 28th of October, Elon Musk officially buys Twitter. Musk kept saying that Twitter was not clear in providing data on the amount of bots on the platform. However, after several talks with the board, he finally received the data. With Elon Musk now being the owner of Twitter, he plans to transform the platform to an "X" application, meaning it will start implementing new features.
